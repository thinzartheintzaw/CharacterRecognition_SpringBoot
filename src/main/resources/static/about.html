About Easy OCR

EasyOCR is an innovative Optical Character Recognition (OCR) application designed to simplify the process of extracting text from images. Whether you need to digitize documents, extract text from images, or convert scanned files into editable text, EasyOCR provides a convenient solution.

OCR at glance

OCR

OCR is sophisticated technology which convert image based text to processable text in different languages. In today's evolving world OCR extraction has become very much important to world leaders. From the job of content writing, content creation, feature extraction from the text of images is very much important. It is an automated process where various machine learning and AI models are used to achieve the goal.

How it works

Steps

Upload Image: Simply upload the image containing the text you want to extract.
Choose Language: Choose the language (English, Myanmar).
Process Text: Our OCR engine will analyze the image and extract the text automatically.
Review and Copy: Review the extracted text and make any necessary copy for further work.
